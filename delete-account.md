Users of the Syrian Digital School app can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

## How to request account deletion

To delete your account, send an email request to:

[sds2025sy@gmail.com](mailto:sds2025sy@gmail.com)

Please include:

* Your phone number or login identifier
* The subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

## What data will be deleted

When your request is processed, we will delete:

* Your name
* Phone number
* Login information
* Learning progress and activity data
* Associated user account records

## Data retention

Some limited technical records may be retained temporarily for legal, security, and fraud-prevention purposes. These records are automatically deleted after a reasonable retention period.

## Processing time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 days.
